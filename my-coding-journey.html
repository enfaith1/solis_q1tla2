MY CODING JOURNEY

My programming journey began with Java, as it was the first programming language my school taught us in the academic year 2024 - 2025. I spent hours in NetBeans IDE writing my first lines of code. I figured out how loops worked, debugged errors, and understood basic concepts like classes and inheritance. There were times when my code refused to run, but most of the time, I was able to find solutions.

I also tried Python in VS Code. For me, Python is more readable than Java. Python felt more flexible and less wordy. I also took a small step into web development. I relearned parts of HTML and CSS. I loved building simple web pages and styling them.

Additionally, I studied the surface of FFmpeg. I experimented with basic video and audio processing commands. I explored AutoHotkey for automating simple tasks on Windows and edited scripts to use in an online game.

Beyond programming, I got interested in cryptocurrency and the technology behind it. I explored blockchain concepts and I learned how transactions work.

I am excited to keep growing as a developer.

The best part?

This is only the beginning.
